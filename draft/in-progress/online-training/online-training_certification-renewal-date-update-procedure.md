# Certification Renewal Date Update Procedure

## Certification Renewal Date Update Procedure

**Department:** Online Training  
**Policy Owner:** Elaine  
**Source Document:** PBT Certification & Training

---

## Purpose

This policy outlines the procedure for updating the certification renewal date for Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) certified teachers. It ensures that all certification records are accurately maintained, enabling teachers to remain active in the Certified Teachers Directory and continue their professional engagement with PBT. The procedure addresses circumstances where manual intervention is required, such as granting extensions, approving renewals, or correcting renewal dates due to technical or administrative issues.

---

## Scope

This procedure applies to all staff members within the Online Training department who are responsible for managing PBT teacher certifications. It covers all scenarios in which a certification renewal date must be updated, including but not limited to:

- Manual approval of certification renewals
- Granting extensions for missed renewal deadlines
- Correcting renewal dates due to system errors or administrative requests
- Supporting certified teachers who are unable to access the online renewal process

---

## Procedure

### 1. Identifying the Need for a Renewal Date Update

Staff must update a certification renewal date in the following situations:
- A certified teacher has completed the renewal process but the system has not updated their renewal date.
- A certified teacher requests an extension after missing the renewal deadline.
- A certified teacher is unable to access the renewal questionnaires online and requires manual approval.
- An administrative or technical error has resulted in an incorrect renewal date.

### 2. Accessing the Certification Management System

- Log in to the admin portal at admin.pbt.dance.
- Navigate to the "Users" section.
- Locate the relevant teacher by searching for their name or email address.

### 3. Updating the Renewal Date

- Select the teacher’s profile.
- Go to the "Certifications" section within their account.
- Locate the certification that requires a renewal date update.
- Change the renewal date as needed:
  - For standard renewals, extend the date by one year from the previous expiry.
  - For extensions, set the new expiry date according to the agreed extension period.
  - For corrections, enter the accurate renewal date as appropriate.

### 4. Manual Approval of Certification Renewal

- If the teacher has completed the renewal requirements outside the online system, review their submitted documentation or questionnaire responses.
- Upon satisfactory completion, manually approve the renewal in the system and update the renewal date accordingly.

### 5. Communication with the Teacher

- Once the renewal date has been updated, notify the teacher via email that their certification status has been renewed and confirm the new expiry date.
- If the teacher’s profile was previously hidden from the Certified Teachers Directory due to lapsed certification, inform them that their profile will reappear within 24–48 hours.

### 6. Record Keeping

- Document the reason for the renewal date update in the teacher’s account notes for future reference.
- Ensure all updates comply with PBT’s certification and data management standards.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Online Training Staff:** Responsible for identifying when a renewal date update is required, carrying out the update in the certification management system, and communicating with the teacher.
- **Policy Owner (Elaine):** Accountable for overseeing the correct implementation of this procedure, providing guidance on exceptions, and ensuring staff are trained in the process.
- **Certified Teachers:** Responsible for initiating contact if they require an extension or encounter issues with the renewal process.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- If a teacher’s certification has lapsed due to extenuating circumstances (e.g., illness, technical issues), staff are authorised to grant reasonable extensions and update the renewal date accordingly.
- No new or replacement certificate is issued upon renewal; the original certificate remains valid, and the renewal extends its validity in the system.
- All updates must be made in accordance with PBT’s privacy and data protection policies.
