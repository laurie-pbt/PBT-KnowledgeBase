# Invoice Provision Policy

## Invoice Provision Policy

**Department:Compliance
**Policy Owner:** Laurie  
**Origin:** Section 2.3

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the standard procedures and requirements for the provision of invoices to clients of Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) in China. It ensures clarity, consistency, and compliance in handling invoice requests, thereby supporting transparent financial operations and meeting client expectations.

## Scope

This policy applies to all customer service, market, and operations support personnel involved in client interactions and payment processing for PBT China. It covers all scenarios where clients request invoices for payments made via supported channels.

## Policy

### Invoice Eligibility

- Invoices are available for all payments made by clients for PBT courses and related services.

### Required Information

- Clients requesting an invoice must provide the following:
  - Invoice title (发票抬头)
  - Tax identification number (税号)

### Invoice Type and Delivery

- Only electronic invoices will be issued.
- Invoices will be sent to the client via the agreed communication channel (e.g., email, WeChat) after payment confirmation.

### Processing Timeline

- After receipt of the required invoice information and confirmation of payment, staff must issue the electronic invoice within 3 to 5 working days.

### Communication with Clients

- When a client requests an invoice, staff must respond promptly and request the necessary information (invoice title and tax number).
- Staff must inform the client of the expected timeline for invoice issuance (3–5 working days after payment).

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service and Operations Support Staff:**
  - Collect required invoice information from clients.
  - Confirm payment has been received before initiating invoice processing.
  - Communicate clearly with clients regarding invoice procedures and timelines.
  - Ensure electronic invoices are issued within the specified timeframe.

- **Finance Team:**
  - Prepare and issue electronic invoices based on the information provided by customer service staff.
  - Ensure accuracy and compliance with relevant financial regulations.

- **Policy Owner (Laurie):**
  - Oversee implementation and compliance with this policy.
  - Review and update the policy as necessary to reflect operational or regulatory changes.

## Notes / Exceptions

- Invoices are only issued after full payment is received.
- Only electronic invoices are provided; paper invoices are not available.
- If a client fails to provide complete invoice information, the processing timeline may be delayed until all required details are received.
