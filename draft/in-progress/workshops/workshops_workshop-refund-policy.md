# Workshop Refund Policy

# Workshop Refund Policy

Department: Workshops
Owner: Kaisey

## Purpose

This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds, credits, or transfers are granted for workshop cancellations. It ensures clarity and fairness for participants and staff regarding refund eligibility and procedures.

## Scope

This policy applies to all participants registered for workshops offered by Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT).

## Procedures

### Refund Eligibility

- - More than 30 days before the workshop: Full refunds are granted upon cancellation.
- - Between 15 and 30 days before the workshop: No refunds are provided; however, credits or transfers may be offered subject to tutor-specific restrictions.
- - Less than 15 days before the workshop: No refunds, credits, or transfers are granted.
- - On the day of or after the workshop: No refunds are provided.
### Administrative Actions

- - All cancellations and refund requests must be documented.
- - Admin notes and area managers must be updated accordingly to reflect the status of refunds, credits, or transfers.
