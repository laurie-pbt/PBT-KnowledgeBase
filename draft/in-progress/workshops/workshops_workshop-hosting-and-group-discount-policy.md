# Workshop Hosting and Group Discount Policy

## Workshop Hosting and Group Discount Policy

**Department:** Workshops

**Policy Owner:** Kaisey

**Source Document:** Procedure for Customer Service

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the procedures and requirements for hosting Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) workshops at external studios and for providing group discounts to participants. The aim is to ensure consistency, transparency, and fairness in workshop hosting arrangements and group pricing, supporting both customer satisfaction and organisational integrity.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- - All customer service staff handling workshop hosting or group discount inquiries.
- - Area Managers responsible for workshop coordination in their respective regions.
- - Any customer or group interested in hosting a PBT workshop or seeking a group discount for PBT workshops.
- - All PBT Certification Teachers Workshops and PCT Teachers Certification Workshops, regardless of location.
---

## Policy

### 1. Hosting a PBT Workshop at an External Studio

#### 1.1 Initial Inquiry Handling

- - All inquiries about hosting a PBT workshop at a customer’s studio must be forwarded to the appropriate Area Manager based on the studio’s region.
- - If the region is unclear, staff should attempt to determine the location via the customer’s email address or other available information.
#### 1.2 Area Manager Contacts

- - **Australia / New Zealand: **Kaisey (kaisey@pbt.dance)
- - **China / Asia: **Elaine (elaine@pbt.dance)
- - **USA / Canada: **Elise (elise@pbt.dance)
- - **UK / Europe: **Shannon (shannon@pbt.dance)
- - **South America / Mexico:** Coni (coni@pbt.dance)
- - **Other Regions: **Kaisey (kaisey@pbt.dance)
#### 1.3 Workshop Hosting Fees and Terms

- - For groups of up to 16 participants: The workshop fee is USD $2,250 (or equivalent in local currency), covering the workshop delivery.
- - The host is responsible for all expenses related to the Tutor’s flights, accommodation, and transportation.
- - For groups exceeding 16 participants: An additional fee of USD $200 (or local equivalent) per participant applies.
- - All pricing must be confirmed in the relevant currency for the host’s country.
- - The Area Manager will provide all further details, answer questions, and coordinate the booking process with the host.
---

### 2. Group Discount Policy

#### 2.1 Eligibility and Discount Tiers

- - Group discounts are available for in-person and online PBT workshops that are less than 90% sold out.
- - Discounts are based on the number of teachers registering as a group:
- **3 to 6 participants:** 20% discount for each participant.

- **7 to 10 participants:** 30% discount for each participant.

- **More than 10 participants:** The inquiry must be forwarded to the Area Manager and cc kaisey@pbt.dance for special handling and possible custom arrangements.

#### 2.2 Discount Code Creation and Distribution

- - Customer service staff must create a unique coupon code in the workshop admin system corresponding to the applicable discount.
- - The coupon code is to be provided directly to the group organiser for use during the booking process.
#### 2.3 Communication with Customers

- - Staff must respond promptly and enthusiastically to group discount inquiries, providing the appropriate coupon code and clear instructions for use.
- - For groups larger than 10, staff must inform the customer that their request is being escalated to the Area Manager for personalised assistance.
---

## Responsibilities

- - **Customer Service Staff:**
- Forward all workshop hosting inquiries to the correct Area Manager.

- Assess group discount eligibility based on workshop capacity and group size.

- Create and distribute coupon codes as per policy.

- Escalate large group (more than 10) inquiries to the Area Manager and cc kaisey@pbt.dance.

- Ensure all communications are professional, accurate, and aligned with this policy.

- - **Area Managers:**
- Respond to hosting inquiries with full details, including pricing and logistical requirements.

- Handle special arrangements for large groups and custom workshop requests.

- Confirm all workshop hosting fees in the relevant local currency.

- - **Policy Owner (Kaisey):**
- Oversee the implementation and periodic review of this policy.

- Provide guidance and clarification to staff and Area Managers as needed.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- - Group discounts are only available if the workshop is less than 90% sold out at the time of inquiry.
- - All group discounts and hosting fees must be confirmed in the local currency appropriate to the workshop location.
- - For any group or hosting requests that fall outside the standard terms (e.g., exceptionally large groups, unique logistical needs), the Area Manager and policy owner must be consulted for a tailored solution.
- - This policy applies strictly to Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) workshops and must not be used or referenced in relation to any other organisation.
