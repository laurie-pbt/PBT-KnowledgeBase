**Policy Owner:** Kaisey  
**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]  
**Review Date:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Purpose  
The purpose of this policy is to establish clear guidelines for acceptable payment methods for Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) workshops. This ensures consistent, secure, and efficient processing of payments while accommodating different customer needs.

## 2. Scope  
This policy applies to all customers registering for PBT workshops and all staff involved in processing workshop payments.

## 3. Payment Methods and Conditions  

### 3.1 Bank Transfer  
- Bank transfer payments are permitted only if the workshop date is at least 7 days away from the date of payment.  
- The workshop must have less than 90% of available positions filled at the time of payment.  
- Bank transfers are not accepted for workshops where the tutor is Adam or Bev.  
- All bank transfer payments must be accompanied by an invoice issued through the appropriate Zoho account.

### 3.2 Payment Plans  
- Payment plans are available only for workshops scheduled at least 4 weeks in the future.  
- The workshop must not exceed 90% capacity.  
- Payment plans are not available for workshops taught by Adam or Bev.

### 3.3 Multiple Card Payments  
- Payment by multiple credit or debit cards is permitted provided there are still available positions in the workshop.

## 4. Payment Rejection and Manual Registration  
- If a payment is rejected, customers should be advised to contact their bank for resolution or request an invoice for alternative payment methods.  
- Any non-standard payment arrangements require manual registration by staff to ensure accurate record-keeping.

## 5. Responsibilities  
- Staff must verify eligibility for each payment method based on the criteria above before processing payments.  
- Invoices must be generated and tracked through the designated Zoho accounts system.  
- Customers must be informed promptly of any payment issues and guided accordingly.

## 6. Compliance  
Failure to adhere to this policy may result in delays or cancellation of workshop registration.
