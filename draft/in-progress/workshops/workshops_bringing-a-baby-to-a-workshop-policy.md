# Bringing a Baby to a Workshop Policy

**Bringing a Baby to a Workshop Policy**  
**Department:** Workshops  
**Owner:** Kaisey  

**Purpose:**  
To provide guidance on the circumstances under which attendees may bring a baby to a Progressing Ballet Technique workshop, balancing inclusivity with the need to maintain a focused and distraction-free learning environment.

**Policy:**  
While bringing a baby to a workshop is not ideal, it may be accommodated in exceptional circumstances when all other childcare options have been exhausted. Attendees who intend to bring a baby must ensure that the baby does not cause distractions or disruptions to other participants.

**Notification:**  
Attendees planning to bring a baby must inform both the area manager and the workshop tutor in advance to allow appropriate arrangements and support.

**Responsibilities:**  
- **Attendees:** Responsible for managing the baby’s presence to minimize distractions and disruptions.  
- **Area Manager and Tutor:** Responsible for assessing the situation and providing support or guidance as needed.

**Compliance:**  
Failure to comply with this policy may result in the attendee being asked to make alternative childcare arrangements to maintain the quality of the workshop experience for all participants.

**Review:**  
This policy will be reviewed annually or as needed to ensure it remains relevant and effective.
