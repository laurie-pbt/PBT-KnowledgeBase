# Workshop Notes Resend Procedure

## Workshop Notes Resend Procedure

**Department:** Workshops  
**Policy Owner:** Kaisey  
**Policy Origin:** Workshops

---

## Purpose

This policy outlines the procedure for resending workshop notes and equipment lists to participants of Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) workshops. The aim is to ensure that all workshop attendees have timely and reliable access to the materials necessary for full participation and learning, whether they require a resend due to non-receipt, loss, or technical issues.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all staff responsible for workshop administration and customer service within the Workshops department. It covers all scenarios in which a participant requests the workshop notes and/or equipment list to be resent, regardless of workshop type (in-person or online), language, or participant location.

---

## Procedure

### 1. Receiving the Request

- Staff must monitor customer service channels (including customerservice@pbt.dance and Wecom for China) for requests from workshop participants who require the workshop notes or equipment list to be resent.
- Verify the participant’s registration details if necessary to confirm their eligibility for the requested materials.

### 2. Resending Workshop Notes and Equipment List

- Use the designated function in the workshop administration system to resend the workshop notes and equipment list to the participant’s registered email address.
    - This action will automatically send the most current version of the notes and equipment list in the appropriate language.
- If the automated resend function is unavailable or the participant requires notes in a specific language:
    - Navigate to Workshops > File Directory in admin.pbt.dance.
    - Download the relevant workshop notes and equipment list (available under English and non-English tabs).
    - Attach the files to a direct email and send them to the participant’s registered email address.

### 3. Confirmation and Follow-up

- Confirm to the participant that the workshop notes and equipment list have been resent.
- Advise the participant to check their spam or junk email folder if they do not receive the materials within a few minutes.
- If the participant still does not receive the materials, verify the email address on file and resend as needed.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Workshop Administration Staff:** Responsible for actioning all requests to resend workshop notes and equipment lists promptly and accurately, using the correct system procedures.
- **Customer Service Staff:** Responsible for communicating with participants, confirming delivery, and escalating any unresolved issues to the Workshops department or policy owner as necessary.
- **Policy Owner (Kaisey):** Accountable for ensuring the policy is up to date, staff are trained in the procedure, and compliance is maintained.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- If a participant requests workshop notes in a language other than English, staff must ensure the correct language version is provided by selecting the appropriate file from the admin.pbt.dance File Directory.
- For participants in China, ensure communication is conducted via the Wecom channel where appropriate.
- If technical issues prevent access to the admin system, escalate to the policy owner or IT support for resolution.
- This procedure does not cover the initial distribution of workshop notes, only resending upon request.
