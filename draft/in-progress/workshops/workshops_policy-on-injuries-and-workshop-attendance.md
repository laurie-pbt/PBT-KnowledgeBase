# Policy on Injuries and Workshop Attendance

# Policy on Injuries and Workshop Attendance

**Department:** Workshops  
**Owner:** Kaisey  
**Enforced by:** Customer Service  

---

## 1. Purpose  
This policy aims to ensure the safety and well-being of all workshop attendees and tutors by establishing clear guidelines for participation when injuries are present. It promotes transparent communication and appropriate accommodations to support attendees while managing risks effectively.

## 2. Scope  
This policy applies to all individuals attending or instructing workshops within the Workshops department, including attendees and tutors.

## 3. Policy Statements  
- Attendees with injuries are welcome to participate in workshops; however, participation may be limited based on the nature and severity of the injury.  
- Observing the workshop is permitted for those unable to participate fully due to injury.  
- Tutors must be informed of any injuries to provide appropriate support and accommodations.  
- Customers are responsible for consulting their healthcare provider regarding their ability to safely participate and must communicate any physical limitations or injuries to the workshop team.  
- Customer Service will manage workshop availability, waitlists, and provide relevant equipment information, especially for bookings made close to the workshop date.  
- While every effort will be made to accommodate injured attendees, no guarantees can be made regarding participation or risk elimination.

## 4. Responsibilities  

### Attendees  
- Disclose any injuries or physical limitations at the time of booking or at least 24 hours prior to the workshop.  
- Consult their healthcare provider to assess suitability for participation.  
- Communicate any changes in injury status promptly to Customer Service.  

### Tutors  
- Review injury information provided by attendees to determine necessary accommodations or participation restrictions.  
- Support attendees with injuries to the best of their ability during the workshop.  

### Customer Service  
- Ensure injury disclosures are collected and communicated to tutors in a timely manner.  
- Advise attendees on workshop availability, waitlists, and equipment needs.  
- Enforce this policy by managing bookings and communications related to injuries.  

## 5. Procedures  

### Injury Reporting and Communication  
- Attendees must inform Customer Service and tutors of any injuries or physical limitations at the time of booking or at least 24 hours before the workshop.  
- If an injury occurs or worsens after booking but before the workshop, attendees must notify Customer Service immediately.  
- Customer Service will relay injury information to tutors to facilitate assessment and accommodation planning.  

### Workshop Participation  
- Tutors will assess each injury case and determine the extent of participation possible, including the option to observe rather than physically participate.  
- Customer Service will inform attendees of any limitations or accommodations based on tutor assessments.  

### Booking and Equipment  
- Customer Service will verify workshop availability and manage waitlists as necessary.  
- Attendees booking close to the workshop date will be provided with information about any required equipment and accommodations.

This policy will be reviewed periodically to ensure it remains effective and aligned with best practices.
