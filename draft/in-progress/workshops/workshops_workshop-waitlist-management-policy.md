# Workshop Waitlist Management Policy

# Workshop Waitlist Management Policy

**Department:** Workshops  
**Policy Owner:** Kaisey  
**Source Document:** Procedure for Customer Service

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure clear, consistent, and fair management of waitlists for Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) Certification Teachers Workshops. This policy outlines the procedures for handling customer inquiries when a workshop is sold out, the process for adding individuals to the waitlist, and the communication protocols for managing expectations. The goal is to maximise workshop attendance, provide excellent customer service, and maintain transparent communication with prospective participants.

## Scope

This policy applies to all staff involved in the administration, customer service, and management of PBT Certification Teachers Workshops, including but not limited to customer service representatives, area managers, and workshop coordinators. It covers all scenarios where a customer wishes to attend a sold-out workshop, regardless of workshop level or location.

## Policy

### 1. Determining Workshop Availability

- Staff must always verify the current registration status of a workshop when responding to customer inquiries.
- If a workshop is sold out, staff must not offer or promise additional places beyond the established capacity unless specifically authorised by the area manager.

### 2. Waitlist Registration Procedure

- When a customer expresses interest in a sold-out workshop, staff must:
  - Inform the customer that the workshop is currently sold out.
  - Offer to add the customer to the official waitlist.
  - Register the customer’s email address and relevant contact details in the admin system’s waitlist for that specific workshop.
- The following standard communication should be used (personalised as appropriate):

  > Hi [Customer Name],
  >
  > I am so sorry to hear that the workshop you’re interested in is sold out.
  >
  > I have added you to the wait-list and should any positions become available or if we’re able to increase the capacity of the workshop, we will contact everyone on this wait-list.
  >
  > I am so sorry I couldn’t help you any further today.
  >
  > Many thanks,
  > [Your Name]

### 3. Managing the Waitlist

- The waitlist must be maintained in the admin system, ensuring all entries are accurate and up to date.
- If a position becomes available (e.g., due to a cancellation or increased workshop capacity), staff must:
  - Contact individuals on the waitlist in the order they were added (first-come, first-served).
  - Provide clear instructions on how to claim the available position, including any relevant deadlines for response and payment.
  - If the first person on the waitlist does not respond within the specified timeframe, the offer should be extended to the next person on the list.

### 4. Communication and Customer Service

- Staff must communicate promptly and courteously with all customers on the waitlist regarding any changes to their status.
- If a customer inquires about their position on the waitlist or the likelihood of securing a place, staff should provide honest and transparent information, without guaranteeing a spot unless one becomes available.
- If workshop capacity is increased, all customers on the waitlist must be notified simultaneously about the new availability.

### 5. Data Management and Privacy

- All customer information collected for the waitlist must be handled in accordance with PBT’s data privacy and protection policies.
- Staff must ensure that waitlist data is used solely for the purpose of workshop registration and related communications.

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:** Responsible for verifying workshop status, adding customers to the waitlist, maintaining accurate records, and communicating with customers regarding their waitlist status.
- **Area Managers:** Responsible for authorising any increase in workshop capacity and ensuring that waitlist procedures are followed in their region.
- **Policy Owner (Kaisey):** Responsible for overseeing the implementation of this policy, providing training as needed, and reviewing the policy for updates.

## Notes / Exceptions

- If a workshop’s capacity is increased, all customers on the waitlist must be contacted at the same time to ensure fairness.
- No fees are to be collected from customers for being placed on the waitlist.
- This policy applies to all PBT Certification Teachers Workshops, including all levels and regions.
- Any exceptions to this policy must be approved by the policy owner.
