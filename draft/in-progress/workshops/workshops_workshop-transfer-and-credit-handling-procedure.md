# Workshop Transfer and Credit Handling Procedure

## Workshop Transfer and Credit Handling Procedure

**Department:** Workshops  
**Policy Owner:** Kaisey  
**Source:** Workshops

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes clear, fair, and consistent procedures for handling requests to transfer workshop bookings, issue credits for future workshops, and manage related scenarios. It aims to ensure transparency for participants and efficient administration for staff, while supporting the operational needs of Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) workshops globally.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All participants registered for PBT Certification Teachers Workshops and PCT Teachers Certification Workshops (in-person and online).
- All staff involved in the administration, management, and customer service of PBT workshops.
- All workshop locations and regions, including special provisions for participants based in China.

---

## Procedure

### 1. Workshop Transfer Requests

#### 1.1 Transfers More Than 15 Days Before the Workshop
- Participants may transfer their registration to any other available workshop with no penalty.
- If the desired workshop is sold out, a credit will be issued to the participant’s account for future use.
- Staff must process the transfer or credit in the admin system and notify the participant accordingly.

#### 1.2 Transfers Less Than 15 Days but More Than 72 Hours Before the Workshop
- Transfers to another workshop are not permitted within this window.
- Participants may transfer their position to another person by providing the new attendee’s full name, email address, and phone number at least 72 hours before the workshop.
- Alternatively, participants may be offered 12 months of online training (lifetime access for China).
- If the workshop is sold out and the position can be filled from the waitlist, a transfer or credit may be arranged.

#### 1.3 Transfers Less Than 72 Hours Before the Workshop
- Transfers to another workshop are not permitted.
- Participants may transfer their position to another person if they provide the required details before the workshop commences.
- If no replacement is found, 12 months of online training will be offered (lifetime access for China).
- If the workshop is sold out and the position is filled from the waitlist, a transfer or credit may be arranged.

#### 1.4 Extreme Circumstances
- In cases of health emergencies, tragedy, or accidents, staff may approve a transfer regardless of timing, prioritising a customer-focused approach.

---

### 2. Credit Handling

#### 2.1 Credits More Than 15 Days Before the Workshop
- Participants may request a cancellation and receive a credit for any future workshop.
- Credits are valid until redeemed for a future workshop of the participant’s choice.
- Staff must record the credit in the system and inform the area manager.

#### 2.2 Credits Less Than 15 Days but More Than 72 Hours Before the Workshop
- Credits are not generally available within this window.
- Participants may transfer their position to another person or receive 12 months of online training (lifetime access for China).
- If the workshop is sold out and the position is filled from the waitlist, a credit may be issued.

#### 2.3 Credits Less Than 72 Hours / Day of / After the Workshop
- Credits are not available at this stage.
- Participants may be offered 12 months of online training as an alternative (lifetime access for China).

#### 2.4 Extreme Circumstances
- In cases of significant hardship, staff may approve a credit regardless of timing.

---

### 3. Replacement of Attendees

- Participants may transfer their workshop position to another person by notifying staff at least 72 hours before the workshop.
- The new attendee’s full name, email address, and mobile number must be provided.
- Staff must update the registration details in the admin system, send the welcome email to the new attendee, and confirm the transfer with the original participant.

---

### 4. Waitlist and Sold-Out Workshops

- If a workshop is sold out, staff may fill vacated positions from the waitlist.
- If the position is filled, a transfer, credit, or refund may be processed for the original participant, depending on the circumstances and timing.
- Staff must allow sufficient time for waitlisted participants to respond before moving to the next person on the list.

---

### 5. Online Training as an Alternative

- Where a transfer or credit is not available, participants may be offered 12 months of online training (lifetime access for China) as an alternative.
- This applies to requests made less than 15 days before the workshop, within 72 hours, or after the workshop date.

---

### 6. China-Specific Provisions

- For participants based in China, any offer of 12 months of online training must be replaced with lifetime access to online training.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Workshop Administration Staff:**  
  - Process all transfer, credit, and replacement requests in accordance with this policy.
  - Update participant records and communicate outcomes promptly.
  - Notify area managers of all credits and significant changes.
  - Ensure China-specific provisions are applied where relevant.
- **Area Managers:**  
  - Oversee the implementation of this policy within their region.
  - Approve exceptions in extreme circumstances.
  - Support staff in resolving complex cases.
- **Policy Owner (Kaisey):**  
  - Maintain and review this policy to ensure ongoing relevance and compliance.
  - Provide guidance and final approval for exceptions or escalated cases.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- In all cases, PBT aims to act with fairness and empathy. Staff are empowered to approve transfers or credits outside standard policy in cases of significant hardship, health emergencies, or other extreme circumstances.
- All communications with participants must be clear, timely, and documented in the appropriate systems.
- This policy does not cover workshop refunds, which are managed under a separate refund policy.
