# Online vs In-Person Workshop Attendance Policy

### Online vs In-Person Workshop Attendance Policy

**Department:** Workshops  
**Policy Owner:** Kaisey

**Purpose:**  
To clearly define attendance options for Progressing Ballet Technique workshops and ensure customers understand the available formats.

**Scope:**  
This policy applies to all customers registering for PBT workshops.

**Policy:**  
1. Attendance at in-person workshops is strictly limited to physical, on-site participation.  
2. In-person workshops are not accessible online unless explicitly designated and offered as an online event.  
3. Customers unable to attend in-person workshops are encouraged to explore available online training options.  
4. Workshop registration materials and communications will clearly indicate the format (in-person or online) of each workshop.

**Responsibilities:**  
- Workshop coordinators must ensure accurate labeling of workshop formats.  
- Customer service representatives should guide customers to appropriate training options based on their availability.

**Compliance:**  
Failure to adhere to this policy may result in denied access to workshops or registration cancellations.
