# Workshop Refund Handling Procedure

## Workshop Refund Handling Procedure

**Department:** Workshops  
**Policy Owner:** Kaisey  
**Source Document:** Workshops

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the procedures and criteria for handling refund requests for Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) workshops. It ensures clarity, fairness, and consistency in responding to participant cancellations and refund enquiries, while supporting the operational needs of the Workshops department.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All PBT Certification Teachers Workshops and PCT Teachers Certification Workshops, whether in-person or online.
- All staff involved in the administration, management, and customer service of PBT workshops.
- All workshop participants who have registered and paid for a workshop.
- All refund requests, regardless of the participant’s location, except where otherwise specified for China.

---

## Procedure

### 1. General Principles

- Refunds are granted based on the timing of the cancellation relative to the workshop date.
- Where a refund is not possible, alternative options such as credits, transfers, or online training access may be offered.
- Special provisions apply for workshops that are sold out and for participants in China.
- In extreme circumstances (e.g., health emergencies, tragedy, accident), exceptions to standard timeframes may be made to accommodate the participant.

### 2. Refund Eligibility by Notice Period

#### **a. 30 Days or More Before Workshop**
- **Eligibility:** Full refund.
- **Process:** Refund is processed to the original payment method.
- **Timeline:** Funds should appear within 5–7 business days.

#### **b. 15–30 Days Before Workshop**
- **Eligibility:** Refunds are not offered.
- **Alternatives:** Offer a credit for a future workshop, transfer to another workshop, transfer the position to someone the participant knows, or provide 12 months of online training (lifetime access for China).
- **Sold-Out Exception:** If the workshop is sold out and the position can be filled from the waitlist, a full refund or credit may be provided.

#### **c. Less Than 15 Days but More Than 72 Hours Before Workshop**
- **Eligibility:** Refunds and credits are not offered.
- **Alternatives:** The participant may transfer their position to another person, or receive 12 months of online training (lifetime for China).
- **Sold-Out Exception:** If the workshop is sold out and the position can be filled from the waitlist, a refund or credit may be possible.

#### **d. Less Than 72 Hours, Day Of, or After the Workshop**
- **Eligibility:** Refunds, credits, and transfers are not offered.
- **Alternative:** Offer 12 months of online training (lifetime for China).

### 3. Sold-Out Workshops and Waitlist Handling

- If a workshop is sold out, staff should attempt to fill the vacated position from the waitlist.
- If the position is filled, a full refund or credit may be issued to the original participant.
- The more notice provided, the higher the likelihood of filling the position and issuing a refund.

### 4. China-Specific Provisions

- For participants in China, where 12 months of online training is offered as an alternative, lifetime access is provided instead.

### 5. Extreme Circumstances

- In cases of serious health concerns, tragedy, or accidents, staff are authorised to offer refunds, credits, or transfers regardless of the timing of the request.
- The Workshops department is customer-focused and will always strive to accommodate participants facing genuine hardship.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Workshops Department Staff:**  
  - Assess refund requests in accordance with this policy.
  - Communicate clearly with participants regarding their eligibility and available options.
  - Process refunds, credits, and transfers in the workshop administration system as appropriate.
  - Attempt to fill vacated positions from the waitlist when applicable.
  - Offer online training access as an alternative when refunds or credits are not possible.
  - Escalate exceptional cases to the area manager or policy owner as needed.

- **Area Managers:**  
  - Approve exceptions in extreme circumstances.
  - Oversee the implementation of this policy within their region.
  - Ensure that all staff are trained in the correct procedures.

- **Policy Owner (Kaisey):**  
  - Maintain and update this policy.
  - Provide guidance and final decisions on complex or disputed refund cases.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- **China:** Lifetime online training access is provided in place of 12 months’ access where relevant.
- **Extreme Circumstances:** Staff may override standard timeframes to provide a refund, credit, or transfer in cases of serious illness, tragedy, or accident.
- **Sold-Out Workshops:** Refunds or credits may be possible if the position is filled from the waitlist, regardless of standard timing restrictions.
- **Processing Timeline:** Refunds should be processed promptly and participants advised that funds may take 5–7 business days to appear, depending on their bank.

---

**Contact:** For all refund enquiries, participants should email customerservice@pbt.dance. Staff should refer to this policy for guidance and escalate to the area manager or policy owner if unsure.
