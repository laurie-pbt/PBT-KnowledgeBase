# Workshop Transfer Policy

# Workshop Transfer Policy

**Department:** Workshops  
**Owner:** Kaisey

## Purpose  
This policy establishes the guidelines and procedures for transferring workshop registrations within Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT). It ensures fair and consistent handling of transfer requests while maintaining operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

## Scope  
This policy applies to all attendees registered for PBT workshops. It covers transfer requests made by attendees and the administrative handling of such requests.

## Policy  
Transfers of workshop registrations are permitted under the following conditions:

- Transfer requests must be submitted more than 15 days prior to the originally scheduled workshop date.
- The desired workshop to which the attendee wishes to transfer must have available space.
- Transfer requests made within 15 days of the workshop date are not permitted by PBT; however, the registered attendee may transfer their spot to another individual.
- Spot transfers to another individual must be completed no less than 72 hours before the workshop start time.
- Transfers or spot transfers are not allowed within 72 hours of the workshop.
- In cases where no transfer is possible within 72 hours, a coupon for online training may be offered at the discretion of PBT.

## Procedures

1. **Transfer Requests > 15 Days Before Workshop:**
   - Attendee submits a transfer request to PBT.
   - PBT verifies availability in the desired workshop.
   - If available, PBT processes the transfer and updates registration records.
   - Admin notes are updated to reflect the transfer.
   - Area managers are informed as appropriate.

2. **Transfer Requests ≤ 15 Days Before Workshop:**
   - Transfers are not accepted by PBT.
   - Attendee may transfer their spot to another individual.
   - The new attendee’s details must be submitted at least 72 hours before the workshop.
   - Admin notes are updated accordingly.
   - Area managers are informed as appropriate.

3. **Transfers Within 72 Hours of Workshop:**
   - No transfers or spot transfers are permitted.
   - PBT may offer a coupon for online training to the attendee.
   - Admin notes are updated.
   - Area managers are informed as appropriate.

## Responsibilities  
- **Attendees:** Must submit transfer requests or spot transfer details within the specified timeframes.  
- **PBT Administration:** Responsible for processing transfer requests, updating records, issuing coupons, and communicating with area managers.  
- **Area Managers:** Stay informed of transfers to manage workshop logistics effectively.

## Definitions  
- **Transfer:** Changing the registered workshop to another available workshop managed by PBT.  
- **Spot Transfer:** Transferring the registered workshop spot to another individual.

## Review  
This policy will be reviewed annually or as needed to ensure relevance and effectiveness.
