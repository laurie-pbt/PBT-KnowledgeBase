# Customer-Facing Surfaces Monitoring Policy

# Customer-Facing Surfaces Monitoring Policy

**Department:** Customer Service  
**Policy Owner:Ruby
**Origin:** "Customer-Facing Surfaces We Monitor"

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that all customer-facing surfaces used by Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) China are actively and consistently monitored during official service hours. This policy aims to provide customers with timely, accurate, and professional support across all relevant communication channels, thereby upholding PBT’s standards of service, protecting staff boundaries, and ensuring a seamless customer experience.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all staff assigned to the China Customer Service team, including those supporting workshops, marketing, and shift-based customer service operations. It covers all scenarios where staff are responsible for monitoring and responding to customer interactions on any official PBT China customer-facing surface during their designated shifts.

---

## Policy

### 1. Surfaces to Monitor

While on shift, staff must actively monitor and respond to customer interactions across the following surfaces:

#### 1.1 Primary Live Service Surface
- **Main WeCom customer service channel** (official 1:1 support gateway)

#### 1.2 Group and Community Surfaces
- Workshop groups
- PBT China Community Group
- PBT Workshop Interest Group
- RedNote groups
- Social media groups (e.g., WeChat, RedNote)
- Social media direct messages and comments
- Any other live customer chat groups or channels requiring monitoring

#### 1.3 Legacy Surfaces
- Old customer service WeChat phone / personal WeChat account (e.g., Ruby)
- Personal WeCom contacts from historical staff relationships
- Other older routes still being used by customers

### 2. Monitoring and Response

- When on shift, staff are responsible for monitoring and responding across all the above surfaces.
- The order of priority is:
  1. **Monitor and reply:** Address direct customer questions, complaints, booking issues, or confusion as the first priority.
  2. **Nurture and support conversion:** Once important messages have been handled, staff may nurture relationships and support conversion through warm, helpful engagement.

### 3. Shift Coverage

- During official customer service hours, at least one staff member must be actively covering all customer-facing surfaces.
- Staff must have the following open and visible at all times while on shift:
  - Main WeCom customer service
  - Workshop groups
  - PBT China Community Group
  - PBT Workshop Interest Group
  - RedNote groups/channels
  - Social media inboxes/comments requiring support
  - Alice (internal support tool)
  - Current workshop schedule

### 4. Handling Legacy and Personal Contact

- If a customer contacts via a legacy or personal route:
  - Assist the customer politely and resolve their issue where possible.
  - Redirect them to the main WeCom customer service channel for future enquiries.
  - Staff must not encourage ongoing use of personal channels or promote their own personal QR codes.

### 5. Group and Community Engagement

- Staff should encourage customers to join relevant groups (workshop, community, or interest groups) when appropriate.
- Workshop groups are used for practical updates, reminders, and support relating to workshops.
- Community and interest groups are for ongoing engagement, support, and nurturing leads.

### 6. Social Media and RedNote Monitoring

- Staff on shift must monitor all assigned RedNote and social media groups, comments, and direct messages.
- This is a core part of customer service coverage and not optional.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
  - Monitor and respond to all relevant customer-facing surfaces during assigned shifts.
  - Ensure all required channels and groups are open and visible.
  - Prioritise direct customer issues before nurturing or engagement activities.
  - Redirect customers from legacy or personal channels to official routes.
  - Encourage group participation where appropriate.

- **Shift Lead/Manager:**  
  - Ensure shift coverage is maintained and all surfaces are monitored.
  - Support staff in managing high-volume periods or coverage gaps.
  - Oversee handover of unresolved cases to the next on-shift staff member.

- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
  - Maintain and update this policy as needed.
  - Ensure all staff are trained and aware of their monitoring responsibilities.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- During periods of unusually high customer volume, more than one staff member may be active on the main WeCom channel and other surfaces. All staff must maintain consistent and professional communication.
- If a staff member is unable to cover their assigned shift due to illness, leave, or another reason, they must notify the team as early as possible and ensure coverage is arranged.
- Staff should use judgement regarding handover at shift end; unresolved cases must be handed over directly to the next staff member with all relevant context.

---

This policy is effective immediately and applies to all current and future members of the PBT China Customer Service team.
