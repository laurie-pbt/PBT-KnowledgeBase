# Official 1:1 Service Channel Policy

## Official 1:1 Service Channel Policy

**Department:** Customer Service  
**Policy Owner:Ruby
**Origin:** Official 1:1 Service Channel

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the official procedures and expectations for 1:1 customer service interactions in PBT China, ensuring all customer enquiries are managed through the approved WeCom channel. The policy aims to deliver consistent, professional, and reliable support, protect staff boundaries, and maintain centralised communication, thereby improving customer experience and operational efficiency.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all PBT China customer service staff, workshop support, China marketing support, and any team members covering morning or evening shifts. It governs all 1:1 customer service interactions with customers in China, including but not limited to direct enquiries, support requests, and escalation processes.

---

## Policy

### 1. Official 1:1 Service Channel

- The main WeCom customer service gateway is the only official channel for 1:1 customer support in China.
    - Official main channel: [https://work.weixin.qq.com/kfid/kfc6c83c89c8d2ce6dd](https://work.weixin.qq.com/kfid/kfc6c83c89c8d2ce6dd)
- All customer enquiries must be directed to and handled through this channel as the default and preferred route.
- Staff must not encourage or allow customers to use personal accounts for ongoing support except where operationally necessary, and must redirect them to the official channel at the earliest opportunity.

### 2. Rationale

- Centralising support in the official channel ensures:
    - Customers contact PBT, not individual staff.
    - Consistent service and easier handover between staff.
    - Reduced risk of customers contacting staff at inappropriate times.
    - Protection of staff privacy and work-life boundaries.
    - Improved visibility and accountability for customer interactions.

### 3. Channel Hierarchy

- The order of preferred customer contact is:
    1. Main WeCom channel (primary)
    2. Official groups (secondary)
    3. Personal accounts (only if necessary, and then redirect to official channel)

### 4. Handling Contacts via Other Paths

- **Old personal WeChat customer service phone:**  
    - Respond politely and assist as needed.
    - Clearly direct the customer to the main WeCom channel for future enquiries.
    - Example: “Of course, I’m happy to help with this. For future support, please use our official PBT China customer service channel so the team can assist you more quickly and consistently.”
- **Staff personal WeCom:**  
    - Assist if the matter is already in progress.
    - Politely explain that future support should go through the official channel.
    - Provide the official QR/link.
    - Example: “I’m happy to help you with this today. For future questions, please contact us through our official PBT China customer service channel so the team can support you quickly. I’ll send you the link here.”
- **Staff must not:**
    - Promote their own personal QR codes casually.
    - Encourage customers to message them directly at all hours.
    - Allow private contact habits to replace the official system.

### 5. Shift Coverage and Handover

- During official customer service hours, at least one staff member must actively cover the main WeCom channel.
- Staff on shift are responsible for monitoring and responding to all relevant customer-facing surfaces, with the main WeCom channel as the highest priority.
- At the end of a shift:
    - The active representative must ensure proper handover using the WeCom channel assignment process.
    - Do not rely solely on WeCom working hours settings; manual reassignment is required.
    - If a customer enquiry can be resolved within 5–10 minutes, staff should complete the conversation before handing over.
    - Only unresolved cases require handover, which must be communicated directly and clearly to the next representative.
- In busy periods, more than one staff member may be active in the main channel, but communication must remain consistent and professional.
- If a staff member cannot cover their shift, they must notify the team as early as possible and arrange coverage.

### 6. Customer Service Principles for 1:1 Interactions

- Staff must:
    - Be friendly, calm, helpful, professional, and solution-led.
    - Not argue with customers or use blunt, annoyed, or dismissive language.
    - Avoid guessing; if unsure, confirm answers or escalate internally.
    - Use positive, warm, and helpful language.
    - Minimise personal ownership of customer relationships.
    - Support and guide customers towards appropriate PBT offerings where relevant.

### 7. Monitoring and Tools

- While on shift, staff must have open:
    - Main WeCom customer service channel.
    - Workshop groups.
    - PBT China Community Group.
    - PBT Workshop Interest Group.
    - RedNote groups/channels.
    - Social media inboxes/comments requiring support.
    - Alice (internal tool).
    - Current workshop schedule.
- This ensures prompt, informed, and effective responses.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
    - Adhere to this policy at all times.
    - Direct all 1:1 customer support through the official WeCom channel.
    - Redirect customers from personal or legacy channels to the official channel.
    - Ensure proper handover at the end of each shift.
    - Monitor all assigned customer-facing surfaces during shifts.
- **Shift Leads/Managers:**  
    - Ensure shift coverage and compliance with handover procedures.
    - Support staff in managing high volume or escalated cases.
- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
    - Maintain and update this policy as required.
    - Oversee enforcement and address any breaches.
- **Escalation Owners:**  
    - Handle escalated customer complaints or issues as per internal escalation paths.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- Personal accounts may be used for customer support only when operationally necessary, and only as a temporary measure before redirecting to the official channel.
- In exceptional operational circumstances, staff may be contacted outside normal hours, but this should be minimised and arranged in advance where possible.
- Legacy channels should be phased out; customers using these must be transitioned to the official WeCom channel as soon as practical.

---

**This policy is mandatory for all PBT China customer service staff and is essential for maintaining a professional, consistent, and effective customer support environment.**
