# Official Customer Service Hours and Roster

**Department:** Customer Service  
**Policy Owner:Ruby
**Origin:** "Official Customer Service Hours" section

## Purpose

This policy establishes the official customer service operating hours and staff roster for Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) China. It ensures reliable, consistent, and professional support for customers, while protecting staff wellbeing and clarifying shift responsibilities. The policy aims to provide clear expectations for shift coverage, handover, and monitoring of all customer-facing channels.

## Scope

This policy applies to all PBT China customer service staff, including those providing workshop support, China marketing support, and morning/evening shift coverage. It covers all scenarios involving direct customer support, group and community management, and escalation procedures during official service hours.

## Policy

### Official Customer Service Hours

- **Monday to Friday:** 9:30am to 10:00pm China time

During these hours, at least one staff member must be actively covering customer service.

**Outside these hours:**
- The WeCom internal knowledge base bot may respond to enquiries.
- Live staff are not expected to provide active support unless specifically arranged.
- Staff must not be disturbed when off shift except in exceptional operational circumstances.

The goal is to provide reliable customer service while respecting staff time off.

### Shift Coverage and Handover

#### Shift Coverage

- The assigned staff member must be actively monitoring and responding to all relevant customer-facing channels during their shift.
- If customer volume is unusually high, additional team members may be asked to assist temporarily in the main WeCom channel.
- More than one staff member may be active during busy periods, but all must maintain consistent and professional communication.

#### Handover Rules

- **Manual Channel Assignment:** WeCom routing must be handled manually. Do not rely solely on WeCom working hours settings; ensure the next representative is added and the current representative removes themselves from chat assignment.
- **End-of-Shift Process:** At the end of a shift, the active representative must:
  - Complete any ongoing conversations where possible, especially if the matter can be resolved within 5–10 minutes.
  - For unresolved cases, directly message the next representative, providing:
    - The customer’s identity
    - A clear explanation of the issue
    - Relevant context
    - Confirmation that the next representative has received and understood the handover
- **Leave/Sickness/Unavailable Shift:** If a staff member cannot cover their assigned shift, they must:
  - Notify the team as early as possible
  - Request coverage from the team
  - Ensure the shift is picked up by another staff member
  - Not assume coverage will happen automatically

### Monitoring Customer-Facing Channels

While on shift, staff must have open and actively monitor:
- Main WeCom customer service channel
- Workshop groups
- PBT China Community Group
- PBT Workshop Interest Group
- RedNote groups/channels
- Social media inboxes/comments requiring support
- Alice (internal assistant)
- Current workshop schedule

Staff are responsible for monitoring and responding across these surfaces, prioritising direct customer enquiries and urgent issues.

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
  - Must adhere to the official hours and roster.
  - Are responsible for active coverage, monitoring all assigned channels, and following handover procedures.
  - Must notify the team and arrange coverage if unable to attend their shift.

- **Head of Customer Service China (Ruby):**  
  - Oversees shift allocation and ensures roster compliance.
  - Manages escalation of customer complaints and operational issues as per internal escalation paths.

- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
  - Maintains and updates this policy as needed.
  - Ensures all staff are aware of and comply with the policy.

## Notes / Exceptions

- Staff are not expected to provide support outside official hours unless specifically arranged.
- During exceptional operational needs, staff may be contacted outside standard hours, but this should be rare and justified.
- The roster may be adjusted as needed to accommodate staff availability, leave, or changes in operational requirements, with appropriate notice and communication to the team.
