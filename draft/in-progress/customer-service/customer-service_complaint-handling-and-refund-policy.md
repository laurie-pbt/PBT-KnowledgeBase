# Complaint Handling and Refund Policy

## Complaint Handling and Refund Policy

**Department:** Customer Service  
**Policy Owner:Ruby
**Origin:** 4.1 投诉处理机制 / 4.2 退款与改期政策话术  
**Last Updated:** April 2025

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the standards and procedures for handling customer complaints and refund requests at Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) China. Its aim is to ensure all customer concerns are addressed promptly, fairly, and consistently, thereby maintaining high levels of customer satisfaction and trust.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All customer service, market, and operations support personnel handling customer interactions.
- All customer complaints and refund requests received via official PBT China channels, including WeChat Official Account, Xiaohongshu, website forms, corporate WeChat, and email.
- All PBT China courses and related services.

## Policy

### Complaint Handling

1. **Acknowledgement and Initial Response**
   - Staff must respond to all customer complaints within 2 hours of receipt on all platforms.
   - During peak periods (such as promotions or course registration), the response time must be within 1 hour.
   - The initial response must acknowledge the complaint, express understanding, and assure the customer that the matter will be followed up.

2. **Internal Review and Follow-up**
   - Staff must calm the customer and commit to investigating the issue.
   - The complaint must be discussed internally to determine an appropriate solution.
   - A formal response must be provided to the customer within 48 hours of the initial complaint.

3. **Resolution and Compensation**
   - If the issue is determined to be the responsibility of PBT, staff may offer one or more of the following as compensation:
     - Course rescheduling or postponement
     - Discount vouchers
     - Partial refund or difference compensation
   - All compensation offers must be communicated clearly and documented.

4. **Serious or Public Complaints**
   - In cases where negative feedback is public and may affect PBT’s reputation, staff must:
     - Immediately take screenshots for record-keeping and notify the operations supervisor.
     - Privately contact the customer to apologise sincerely and listen to feedback.
     - Propose compensation or a solution.
     - Where necessary, issue an official response from the PBT China account.

### Refund Policy

1. **Refund Requests**
   - When a customer requests a refund (e.g., due to being unable to attend a course), staff must respond according to the official website policy.
   - If a dispute arises, staff may consult with colleagues or supervisors to determine a flexible solution.

2. **Communication**
   - Staff must use polite, positive, and affirmative language when discussing refunds.
   - Negative phrasing (such as "cannot" or "no way") must be avoided; instead, staff should suggest alternatives or solutions.

3. **Documentation**
   - All refund requests and outcomes must be recorded, including customer name, contact details, course information, payment status, and resolution.

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
  - Respond to complaints and refund requests within the required timeframes.
  - Document all interactions and resolutions accurately.
  - Escalate complex or unresolved issues to supervisors as needed.

- **Operations Supervisor:**  
  - Oversee the complaint and refund process.
  - Approve compensation or refunds where required.
  - Ensure all public complaints are managed appropriately.

- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
  - Maintain and update this policy as needed.
  - Provide training and guidance to staff on complaint handling and refund procedures.

## Notes / Exceptions

- In cases of disputes or special circumstances not covered by the standard policy, staff may consult with colleagues or supervisors to determine an appropriate and flexible solution.
- This policy will be reviewed and updated quarterly in response to market changes and customer feedback.
