# Technical Issue Escalation Procedure

## Technical Issue Escalation Procedure

**Department:** Customer Service  
**Policy Owner:** Christian  
**Origin:** Account Management & Membership

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes a clear and consistent procedure for escalating technical issues encountered by customers or staff within the Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) online platform, app, and related digital services. Its aim is to ensure prompt, accurate, and effective resolution of technical problems, minimising disruption to customer experience and maintaining high standards of service.

---

## Scope

This procedure applies to all Customer Service staff handling customer enquiries related to technical issues, including but not limited to account access, platform functionality, app performance, and digital content delivery. It covers all customer-facing channels (email, live chat, website contact forms) and internal action steps required for escalation. The policy is relevant to issues arising from the PBT website, online membership platform, PBT app, and related digital services.

---

## Procedure

### 1. Identification of Technical Issues

Customer Service staff must determine whether a customer enquiry constitutes a technical issue. Technical issues include, but are not limited to:

- Inability to log in or access accounts after standard troubleshooting
- Platform or app crashes, errors, or malfunctions
- Problems with video streaming, content access, or feature availability
- Issues with applying discount codes or redeeming vouchers that cannot be resolved through manual intervention
- Any other malfunction or unexpected behaviour of the PBT website, app, or digital services

### 2. Initial Troubleshooting

Before escalation, staff must guide the customer through standard troubleshooting steps as appropriate, such as:

- Clearing browser cache and cookies
- Trying a different browser or device
- Force-closing and reopening the app
- Reinstalling the app from the App Store or Google Play
- Verifying correct login credentials

If these steps do not resolve the issue, proceed to escalation.

### 3. Collecting Information

For effective escalation, staff must gather the following information from the customer:

- Detailed description of the issue
- Steps taken prior to encountering the issue
- Device type and model (for app issues)
- Operating system version (for app or device-specific issues)
- Screenshots and/or screen recordings illustrating the problem (where possible)
- Any error messages received

### 4. Escalation to the Development Team

Once a technical issue is confirmed and relevant information has been collected:

- Escalate the issue to the development team via the designated internal channel (e.g., support ticket system, email, or internal communication platform).
- Include all collected details and attachments (screenshots, recordings) in the escalation.
- Clearly state the urgency if the issue is preventing access to essential services or affecting multiple users.

### 5. Customer Communication

- Acknowledge receipt of the customer’s report and inform them that the issue has been escalated to the technical team.
- Provide an estimated timeframe for follow-up, if available.
- Advise the customer of any temporary workarounds (e.g., accessing content via the website if the app is malfunctioning).
- Update the customer as soon as a resolution or further information is received from the development team.

### 6. Documentation

- Record all actions taken, including troubleshooting steps, escalation details, and customer communications, in the relevant customer service system or ticket.
- Ensure that all escalated issues are tracked until resolution and closure.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
  - Identify technical issues, perform initial troubleshooting, collect necessary information, escalate issues as per procedure, and maintain clear communication with customers.
  - Document all actions and updates related to each technical issue.

- **Development Team:**  
  - Investigate escalated technical issues, provide timely updates and resolutions, and communicate any required follow-up actions to Customer Service.

- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
  - Ensure staff are trained on this procedure, monitor compliance, and review the effectiveness of the escalation process.
  - Liaise with the development team to address recurring or critical technical issues.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- For customers based in China, technical support and escalation may require coordination via the Wecom customer service channel.
- If a technical issue is identified as urgent or affects a significant number of users, staff must flag the escalation as high priority and notify the policy owner immediately.
- This procedure does not cover non-technical enquiries (e.g., billing, membership queries, workshop bookings), which are handled under separate policies.
