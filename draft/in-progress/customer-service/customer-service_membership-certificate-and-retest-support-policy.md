# Membership, Certificate and Retest Support Policy

## Membership, Certificate and Retest Support Policy

**Department:** Customer Service  
**Policy Owner:Ruby

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the standards and procedures for supporting customers with Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) memberships, certificates, and retest processes. It ensures that all customer service staff provide accurate, timely, and helpful assistance, fostering a positive and professional customer experience while maintaining operational consistency.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all PBT China customer service staff, including those supporting workshops, marketing, and shift-based customer service. It covers all scenarios involving customer enquiries and support requests related to:

- Membership access and issues
- Certificate delivery and queries
- Retest procedures and support

---

## Policy

### 1. Membership Support

- Staff must be able to assist customers with basic membership enquiries, including activation, access, and general information.
- If a customer cannot access their membership after following standard support steps, staff must escalate the issue to Yang. If unresolved, escalate further to Lachlan.
- Staff must not attempt to resolve technical or system-level membership issues beyond their training or authority.

### 2. Certificate Support

- All certificates are issued electronically and are normally sent to the customer’s email address within approximately 48 hours of successful workshop completion.
- When a customer enquires about their certificate, staff must:
  - Confirm whether the workshop has been completed properly.
  - Verify the expected certificate delivery timeframe.
  - Check and confirm the customer’s correct email address if necessary.
  - Escalate the enquiry if the certificate has not been received within the expected timeframe or if there are any irregularities.
- Staff must not manually issue certificates or make exceptions without proper authorisation.

### 3. Retest Support

- Customers who do not pass a workshop will receive a retest or result notice.
- The standard retest process requires:
  - Submission of written answers as specified.
  - Submission of a video component as specified.
- When a customer is confused or enquires about the retest process, staff must:
  - Calmly explain the requirements for both written and video submissions.
  - Ensure the customer understands the process and what is expected.
  - Direct the customer to the correct internal support owner if further clarification or support is needed.
- Staff must not alter retest requirements or deadlines without authorisation.

### 4. Teacher Listing and Profile Completion

- For teacher listing, customers must complete their profile accurately, including city and region information.
- Staff should guide customers through the profile completion process as needed.

### 5. General Support Principles

- All customer enquiries must be handled in a friendly, calm, helpful, and professional manner.
- Staff must not guess answers. If unsure, staff should confirm information or escalate the enquiry.
- Use positive, solution-led language at all times.
- Customer communication should remain within official PBT channels.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
  - Provide first-line support for all membership, certificate, and retest enquiries.
  - Escalate unresolved or complex issues according to the escalation path:  
    - Membership/access issues: escalate to Yang, then Lachlan if needed.  
    - Certificate/retest issues: escalate to Elaine, then Kaisey if needed.
  - Ensure all customer interactions adhere to PBT’s customer service standards.

- **Escalation Owners:**  
  - Yang: First escalation point for membership/access issues.
  - Lachlan: Secondary escalation for membership/access issues.
  - Elaine: First escalation point for certificate/retest issues.
  - Kaisey: Secondary escalation for certificate/retest issues.

- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
  - Ensure this policy is up to date and enforced.
  - Review and update procedures as needed.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- Some membership or access issues may require escalation beyond the customer service team, depending on technical or system complexities.
- Staff must not make promises or exceptions outside the approved process. Any requests falling outside standard procedures must be escalated for management approval.
- All customer-facing communication should remain centralised through official PBT channels, even if internal escalation involves named individuals.
