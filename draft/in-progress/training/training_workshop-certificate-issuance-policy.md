## Purpose  
This policy establishes the process for issuing certificates to participants of Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) workshops, ensuring timely delivery and consistent communication.

## Scope  
This policy applies to all customers who complete a PBT workshop, whether in-person or online.

## Policy Statement  
Certificates of completion will be issued via email within 72 hours following the conclusion of the workshop. Along with the certificate, customers will receive information about accessing PBT online training resources and the certified teachers directory.

## Procedures  
- Upon completion of a workshop, the Online Training team will verify participant attendance and completion status.  
- Certificates will be generated and emailed to the customer’s registered email address within 72 hours.  
- The email will include:  
  - The digital certificate of completion  
  - Instructions for accessing online training materials  
  - A link to the certified teachers directory  

## Timing  
Certificates must be sent no later than 72 hours after the workshop ends to ensure participants receive timely recognition and resources.

## Communication  
All certificate-related communications will be professional, clear, and include relevant links and instructions to support continued engagement with PBT training.

## Owner and Review  
The Online Training department, led by Kaisey, owns this policy and is responsible for its implementation and review. This policy will be reviewed annually or as needed to reflect changes in process or technology.
