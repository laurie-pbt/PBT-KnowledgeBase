# Merchandising Fridge Use and Maintenance Policy

## Purpose

To ensure the shared fridge used by the Merchandising department is maintained in a clean, organized, and safe manner, supporting food safety and a positive workplace environment.

## Scope

This policy applies to all staff and contractors within the Merchandising department who use the designated fridge in company premises.

## Policy Statement

1. **Permitted Use:**  
   The fridge is for the storage of perishable food, beverages, and merchandising samples only. Personal items must be clearly labeled with the owner’s name and date.

2. **Prohibited Items:**  
   The storage of expired, spoiled, or strong-smelling items (e.g., fish, certain cheeses) is not permitted.

3. **Cleaning Schedule:**  
   The fridge must be cleaned out every Friday by 4:00 PM. Any unclaimed or unlabeled items will be discarded at this time.

4. **Temperature Control:**  
   The fridge temperature must be maintained between 2°C and 5°C. Any issues with temperature should be reported immediately to the Customer Service Team.

5. **Shared Responsibility:**  
   All users are responsible for keeping the fridge tidy and promptly removing their items once expired or no longer needed.

## Exceptions

Requests for exceptions (e.g., temporary storage of special merchandising samples) must be submitted in writing to the Head of Marketing for approval.

## Escalation

Non-compliance should be reported to the Customer Service Team, who will address issues directly with the individual or escalate to the Head of Marketing as needed.

## Review Schedule

This policy will be reviewed annually by the Head of Marketing, with feedback from the Customer Service Team and Merchandising department.
