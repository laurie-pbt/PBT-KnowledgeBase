# Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide clear guidance on the shipping of merchandise for Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT), including approved carriers, shipping rates, and regional procedures. This ensures consistency, transparency, and a high standard of service for customers worldwide.

# Scope

This policy applies to all staff and contractors involved in the sale, fulfilment, and support of PBT merchandise within the Merchandising and Customer Service departments. It covers all global regions where PBT ships products.

# Policy Statement

1. **Approved Carriers**  
   PBT ships globally using the following main logistics partners, selected according to region:
   - United States & Canada: USPS, UPS, FedEx
   - Europe: DHL, UPS Road, AnPost
   - Australia & New Zealand: Australia Post, Couriers Please, Aramex
   - Asia: SF Express, 4PX

2. **Shipping Rates and Quotes**  
   - Shipping rates are determined by destination and parcel weight, calculated automatically at checkout.
   - Larger or wholesale orders may require a custom shipping quote before dispatch.

3. **Regional Shipping Details**  
   - Delivery times, carriers, and rates vary by region and are displayed at checkout.
   - Free shipping thresholds and applicable duties/taxes are set per region and are subject to change based on local regulations.
   - Full regional details, including current rates and carrier options, are maintained in the PBT Store Knowledge Base and must be referenced for accuracy.

4. **Duties and Taxes**  
   - Duties, taxes, and VAT are the responsibility of the recipient unless otherwise specified.
   - Information on applicable duties/taxes for each region is provided at checkout and in the Knowledge Base.

5. **Customer Communication**  
   - Customers must be informed of estimated delivery times, shipping costs, and any duties/taxes at the time of purchase.
   - Tracking information must be provided upon dispatch.

6. **Policy Updates**  
   - Any changes to carriers, rates, or regional procedures must be approved by the Head of Product and communicated to all relevant staff.

# Exceptions

- Exceptions to approved carriers or shipping procedures may only be made with written authorisation from the Head of Product.
- In cases of force majeure or carrier disruption, alternative arrangements may be made as directed by the Head of Product.

# Escalation

- Issues or disputes regarding shipping, rates, or carrier selection should be escalated to the Customer Service Team Lead.
- Unresolved matters may be further escalated to the Head of Product for final determination.

# Review Schedule

This policy will be reviewed annually by the Head of Product in consultation with the Customer Service Team and Merchandising department, or sooner if there are significant changes to shipping partners or regional regulations.
