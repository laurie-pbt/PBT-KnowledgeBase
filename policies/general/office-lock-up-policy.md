# Office Lock-Up Policy

## Purpose

To ensure the security and safety of Progressing Ballet Technique’s office premises by outlining the required procedures for locking up at the end of each working day.

## Scope

This policy applies to all staff members across all departments who may be responsible for closing the office.

## Policy Statement

1. All staff responsible for locking up the office must complete the following standard checklist:
    - Ensure all doors and windows are securely locked.
    - Turn off all lights.
    - Ensure the air conditioning is set to the designated "on" position as per office guidelines.
    - Activate any security alarms or systems as required.

2. If a staff member discovers the office has been left unlocked, or notices any security breach, they must immediately contact Laurie.

3. Failure to comply with these procedures may result in disciplinary action in accordance with Progressing Ballet Technique’s staff conduct policies.

## Exceptions

There are no exceptions to this policy.

## Escalation

All incidents or concerns regarding office security should be escalated directly to Laurie.

## Review Schedule

This policy will be reviewed annually by the Head of Compliance, or sooner if required by changes in office security procedures.
