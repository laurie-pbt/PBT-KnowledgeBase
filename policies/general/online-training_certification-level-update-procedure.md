# Certification Level Update Procedure

## Certification Level Update Procedure

**Department:** Online Training  
**Policy Owner:** Elaine  
**Source Document:** PBT Certification & Training

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the standard procedure for updating a teacher’s Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) certification level following successful completion of the required assessments or workshops. The aim is to ensure accurate, timely, and consistent updates to certification records and public listings, supporting the integrity of the PBT certification process and the Certified Teachers Directory.

---

## Scope

This procedure applies to:

- All PBT-certified teachers seeking to progress to a higher certification level (Levels 2 or 3) via the online training platform.
- PBT staff responsible for reviewing assessments, updating certification records, and maintaining the Certified Teachers Directory.
- The Online Training department and relevant administrative personnel.

---

## Procedure

### 1. Eligibility and Assessment Completion

- Teachers must hold a current PBT certification at the preceding level before progressing to the next (e.g., Level 1 before Level 2).
- To apply for a higher certification level, teachers must:
  - Hold an active Teacher account on the PBT online platform.
  - Complete all required assessment sections for the desired level within the “Assessment” section of their account. This includes:
    - Answering questions on specific PBT exercises.
    - Submitting video demonstrations of teaching selected exercises.
- Each completed section earns a “badge” on the teacher’s profile.

### 2. Review and Approval

- Submitted assessments and videos are reviewed by the PBT team.
- Feedback is provided within 7 days of submission.
- Teachers must wait for approval of each section before proceeding to the next.
- All required badges for the certification level must be collected before the level can be updated.

### 3. Certification Level Update

- Once all assessment badges are earned and approved:
  - The responsible staff member must update the teacher’s certification level in the administration portal (admin.pbt.dance) as follows:
    1. Navigate to Users > Find customer > Certifications.
    2. Click the “+” button to add the new certification level.
- The teacher’s profile in the Certified Teachers Directory will be updated to reflect the new certification level within 24–48 hours.

### 4. Communication

- Notify the teacher by email that their certification level has been updated and will appear in the Certified Teachers Directory within 24–48 hours.
- If any issues arise or further information is required, contact the teacher promptly via their registered email address.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Teachers:** Responsible for completing all assessments and submitting required materials for the desired certification level.
- **Online Training Department Staff:** Responsible for:
  - Reviewing assessment submissions.
  - Providing timely feedback.
  - Updating certification levels in the admin portal upon successful completion.
  - Communicating status updates to teachers.
- **Policy Owner (Elaine):** Accountable for ensuring the procedure is followed, maintaining accuracy in certification records, and overseeing the Certified Teachers Directory.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- Certification level updates are only processed after all required badges and assessments for the level are completed and approved.
- If a teacher’s certification has lapsed or their account setup is incomplete, the certification level update may be delayed until the issue is resolved.
- In cases where a teacher has changed their registered email address, staff must verify the teacher’s identity and transfer certifications to the new account before updating the certification level.
- Any requests or issues outside the standard process should be escalated to the policy owner for review.
