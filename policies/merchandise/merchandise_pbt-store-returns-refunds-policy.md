# PBT Store Returns & Refunds Policy

## Purpose

This policy outlines the rules and procedures for returns, exchanges, and refunds for purchases made through the Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) Store (pbtstore.com). It ensures compliance with local consumer protection laws and provides clear guidance for customers and staff.

## Scope

This policy applies to all purchases made via the PBT Store worldwide, with regional variations as required by local consumer law. Where a customer’s region is not explicitly addressed, customer support should be contacted for guidance.

## Policy Statement

### 1. Return Categories

#### 1.1 Change-of-Mind Returns
- Eligible within 30 days of purchase.
- Outcomes may include refund, exchange, or store credit.
- Items must be unused, with tags attached (if applicable), and in original packaging.
- Change-of-mind refunds are not guaranteed and may depend on product category or promotion conditions.

#### 1.2 Defective or Faulty Products
- All products include a 12-month warranty against defects.
- **Major Defect:** Customer entitled to a full refund or replacement.
- **Minor Defect:** PBT will repair or replace the product.
- Customers must report defects within 30 days of identifying the issue, providing proof of purchase, a description, and photos if applicable.

### 2. Proof of Purchase

Proof of purchase is required for all returns, exchanges, and warranty claims. Accepted forms include:
- Receipt
- Bank statement
- Order confirmation email

### 3. Return Process

1. **Contact Support:** Email customerservice@pbt.dance with order details and reason for return.
2. **Receive Instructions:** Customer Service will provide return instructions or authorisation.
3. **Prepare Package:** Securely package the item to ensure protection during transit.
4. **Ship Item:** Use a reliable courier and retain proof of postage until the return is processed.

### 4. Shipping Cost Rules

- **Voluntary Returns (Change of Mind/Exchange):** Customer pays return shipping; PBT pays shipping for any replacement item.
- **Defective Products:** PBT covers all shipping costs for both return and replacement.

### 5. Refund Processing

- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days after the returned item is received.
- Store credit is issued within 3 business days.

### 6. EU/International Cooling-Off Rule

For regions governed by EU consumer law:
- Customers may withdraw from purchase within 14 days of receiving goods, without reason.
- Products must be unused, in original condition, with tags and packaging intact.
- Customer pays return shipping; PBT covers exchange shipping if an exchange is requested.

### 7. Regional Differences

- **Australia:** Governed by Australian Consumer Law (ACL). Major defect: refund or replacement. Minor defect: repair or replacement.
- **United States & Canada:** Policy aligns with FTC Act, Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, and Canadian Competition Act.
- **Japan:** No refunds for change-of-mind; exchanges or store credit available within 30 days.
- **South Korea:** Policy compliant with Consumer Protection Act.

### 8. Customer Support Contact

For all returns and refund enquiries, contact: customerservice@pbt.dance

## Exceptions

There are currently no exceptions for high-value or limited-edition items.

## Escalation

The following scenarios must be escalated to a Customer Service Team Lead:
- Customer claims a major defect.
- Customer cites consumer law rights.
- Warranty claim after a repair attempt.
- Missing proof of purchase.
- Dispute about shipping liability.
