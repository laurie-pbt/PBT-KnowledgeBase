# Returns Policy

Department: Merchandising
Policy Owner: Lachlan
**Source:** Website FAQ and policy material worth importing

## Purpose

This policy establishes the rules and procedures for returns, refunds, and exchanges for products purchased from the Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) Store. It aims to ensure clarity for customers and staff, uphold consumer rights, and maintain product quality and resale standards.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All customers purchasing products from the PBT Store, including individuals, studios, and institutions.
- All PBT Store products, including equipment, bundles, and garments (e.g., Backalast).
- Customer Service staff responsible for handling returns, refunds, and exchanges.
- All regions in which the PBT Store operates, with explicit reference to Australian Consumer Law for Australian customers.

## Policy

### 1. General Returns and Exchanges

- Customers may request a return, refund, or exchange for eligible products purchased from the PBT Store.
- All returns must comply with the eligibility requirements outlined below.
- Returns and exchanges are subject to region-specific consumer protection laws, including Australian Consumer Law for customers in Australia.

### 2. Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a return, refund, or exchange, the product must:
- Have all original tags attached.
- Be in its original packaging.
- Be unwashed and undamaged.
- Be free of marks, stains, or odours.
- Be in a condition suitable for resale.

PBT reserves the right to reject returns that do not meet these conditions.

### 3. Backalast 30-Day Risk-Free Trial

- The Backalast posture garment is covered by a 30-day risk-free trial.
- Customers have 30 calendar days from the date of delivery to try the product.
- If unsatisfied, customers may return the Backalast for a full refund or exchange, provided it meets the eligibility requirements above.

### 4. Faulty or Incorrect Items

- If a product is faulty, damaged on arrival, or incorrect, customers are entitled to a replacement, exchange, or refund in accordance with applicable consumer protection laws.
- Faulty item claims must be supported by evidence (e.g., photographs) and reported to Customer Service as soon as possible after delivery.

### 5. Return Process

1. Customers must contact PBT Customer Service to initiate a return, refund, or exchange request.
2. Customer Service will provide instructions for returning the product, including the return address and any required documentation.
3. Customers are responsible for ensuring the product is securely packaged for return.
4. Upon receipt and inspection of the returned item, Customer Service will notify the customer of the outcome and process the refund or exchange as appropriate.

### 6. Shipping Costs

- Unless the product is faulty or incorrect, customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable except where required by law or in cases of faulty or incorrect items.

### 7. Region-Specific Rights

- For Australian customers, all returns, refunds, and exchanges are managed in accordance with Australian Consumer Law, which provides additional rights and protections.

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
  - Must assess all return requests against the eligibility requirements.
  - Must communicate clearly with customers regarding return procedures and outcomes.
  - Must process refunds and exchanges in a timely manner.
  - Must escalate any disputes or unclear cases to the policy owner.

- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
  - Is responsible for maintaining, reviewing, and updating this policy.
  - Must ensure compliance with regional consumer laws and organisational standards.
  - Must provide guidance and training to Customer Service staff as required.

## Notes / Exceptions

- PBT reserves the right to reject returns that do not meet the stated eligibility conditions (tags, packaging, condition).
- Customised or special-order items may not be eligible for return unless faulty or not as described.
- This policy does not cover shipping delays, customs duties, or taxes, which are addressed in the Shipping Information policy.
- Any exceptions to this policy must be approved by the policy owner.
