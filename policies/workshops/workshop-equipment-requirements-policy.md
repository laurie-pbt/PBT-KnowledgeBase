# Workshop Equipment Requirements Policy

# Workshop Equipment Requirements Policy

**Department:** Workshops  
**Policy Owner:** Kaisey  
**Origin:** Equipment Needed for the Workshop / Procedure for Customer Service

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure all participants in Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) Certification Teachers Workshops are fully informed of the equipment requirements necessary for effective participation. This policy addresses the need for clarity regarding required equipment, procedures for sourcing or borrowing equipment, and guidance for customer service in responding to related enquiries. The aim is to provide a consistent, professional, and supportive experience for all workshop attendees.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All PBT Certification Teachers Workshops and PCT Teachers Certification Workshops, regardless of level or location.
- All workshop participants, including teachers, instructors, and other eligible attendees.
- Customer service staff, area managers, tutors, and workshop organisers responsible for participant communications and workshop logistics.

---

## Policy

### 1. Standard Equipment Requirements

All participants must bring the following equipment to every in-person PBT workshop:

- **Exercise Ball / Fit Ball**
- **3 x Fusion Balls:** Also known as small exercise balls or Pilates balls. If unavailable, toy balls may be used as substitutes. Required size: 20–22 cm (8–9 inches) in diameter.
- **Resistance Band (Theraband):** 3 metres long, medium strength, approximately 15 cm wide.
- **Massage Ball:** 6–8 cm in diameter.
- **Yoga Mat**

Participants are responsible for sourcing and bringing their own equipment unless otherwise specified.

### 2. Equipment Sourcing Guidance

#### a. Workshops More Than 2 Weeks Away (Except South America)
- Participants should be directed to purchase required equipment from the official PBT online store (www.pbtstore.com).
- Customer service must advise participants to allow sufficient delivery time.

#### b. Workshops Less Than 2 Weeks Away or in South America
- Participants should be advised to source equipment locally, as delivery from the PBT store may not arrive in time.
- No reference to the online store should be made for these cases.

### 3. Borrowing Equipment

#### a. General Principle
- PBT does not provide extra equipment for borrowing at workshops, as tutors often travel from other regions and cannot carry spare items.
- Pumps will be available at all workshops so participants can bring balls deflated in their luggage.

#### b. Regional and Timing Considerations

- **Workshops More Than 2 Weeks Away (Areas A, B, C) & No Equipment for Sale at Workshop:**
  - Participants have time to source and bring their own equipment.
  - Advise that all equipment can fit in a carry-on suitcase when deflated.

- **Workshops Less Than 2 Weeks Away (Not Area A) & No Equipment for Sale at Workshop:**
  - Inform participants that borrowing is not possible and equipment cannot be guaranteed for delivery.
  - Advise to bring their own equipment and offer support in identifying local suppliers if possible.

- **Workshops in Area D (at least 72 hours away) & No Equipment for Sale at Workshop:**
  - Same guidance as above: borrowing is not possible, bring own equipment, pumps will be available.

- **Workshops Less Than 3 Days Away & No Equipment for Sale at Workshop:**
  - Customer service must attempt to assist the participant by:
    - Contacting the area manager to check if the tutor can lend equipment.
    - If not possible, researching local sports or department stores near the venue and informing the participant of options.

#### c. Workshops Where Equipment Is Available for Sale
- If equipment will be sold at the workshop, participants may purchase required items on-site.
- No advance sourcing advice is necessary in this case.

### 4. Special Circumstances

- **Last-Minute Bookings (Within 5 Days):** Customer service must inform participants of equipment requirements before booking to ensure they have time to obtain the necessary items.
- **Borrowing Requests:** All requests to borrow equipment must be handled according to the timing and regional guidelines above.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Participants:** Responsible for bringing all required equipment to the workshop unless otherwise notified.
- **Customer Service Staff:** Must provide accurate, timely information on equipment requirements, sourcing options, and borrowing policies based on workshop location and timing.
- **Area Managers:** Responsible for supporting customer service in exceptional cases (e.g., last-minute borrowing requests) and coordinating with tutors regarding possible equipment lending.
- **Tutors:** May be asked by area managers if they are able to lend spare equipment in emergency cases, but are not required to provide extra items.
- **Policy Owner (Kaisey):** Accountable for maintaining and updating this policy and ensuring all staff are informed of any changes.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- **Workshops in South America:** Always advise participants to source equipment locally due to delivery constraints.
- **Workshops Where Equipment Is Sold On-Site:** Participants may purchase equipment at the workshop; advance sourcing is not required.
- **Emergency Borrowing (Less Than 3 Days):** Customer service must make reasonable efforts to assist, but cannot guarantee equipment availability.
- **Deflated Equipment:** Participants are encouraged to bring balls deflated for ease of travel; pumps will be provided at the venue.

---

This policy ensures all participants are adequately prepared for PBT workshops and that staff provide consistent, supportive guidance regarding equipment requirements.
