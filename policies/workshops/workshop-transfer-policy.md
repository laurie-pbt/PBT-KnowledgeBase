# Workshop Transfer Policy

# Workshop Transfer Policy

Department: Workshops

Policy Owner: Kaisey

Effective Date: Friday 6 March 2026

Review Date: 12 months

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the guidelines and procedures for transferring workshop registrations within the Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) programme. It aims to provide clarity and fairness for attendees requesting transfers while ensuring administrative efficiency and minimal disruption to workshop operations.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all individuals registered for PBT workshops, including participants and administrative staff involved in managing workshop registrations and transfers.

## 3. Definitions

- - - Transfer: The process of changing a registered workshop date or transferring a registration to another individual.
- - - Attendee: The individual originally registered for the workshop.
- - - Transfer Request: A formal request made by the attendee to change their workshop registration to a different date or person.
## 4. Transfer Eligibility and Conditions

### 4.1 Transfers More than 15 Days Before Workshop

- - - Attendees may request to transfer their registration to a different workshop date if the request is made more than 15 calendar days prior to the originally scheduled workshop date.
- - - Transfer requests are subject to availability of the desired workshop date.
- - - Transfers will be processed upon confirmation of availability and payment status.
### 4.2 Transfers Within 15 Days but More than 72 Hours Before Workshop

- - - Transfers to a different workshop date are not permitted within 15 days of the workshop.
- - - However, attendees may transfer their registration to another individual, provided the new attendee’s details are submitted at least 72 hours before the workshop start time.
- - - The original attendee remains responsible for ensuring the replacement attendee meets any prerequisites and agrees to the workshop terms.
### 4.3 Transfers Within 72 Hours of Workshop

- - - No transfers of registration or attendee substitution are permitted within 72 hours of the workshop start time.
- - - Attendees unable to attend may be offered a coupon or credit to access online training content, at the discretion of the policy owner or designated manager.
## 5. Transfer Process

### 5.1 Submission

- - - All transfer requests must be submitted in writing via the designated communication channels (e.g., email to the workshops department).
- - - Requests must include the attendee’s full name, original workshop date, desired transfer date or replacement attendee details, and contact information.
### 5.2 Approval and Confirmation

- - - Workshop administration will review transfer requests and confirm approval or denial within 3 business days.
- - - Approved transfers will be updated in the administrative system promptly.
- - - Attendees will receive written confirmation of the transfer outcome.
### 5.3 Notification

- - - Relevant managers and instructors will be notified of all approved transfers to ensure proper workshop preparation.
## 6. Responsibilities

- - - Attendees: Responsible for submitting transfer requests within the specified timeframes and providing accurate information.
- - - Workshop Administration: Responsible for processing transfer requests, updating records, and communicating decisions.
- - - Policy Owner: Responsible for overseeing policy enforcement and handling exceptions.
## 7. Exceptions

- - - Any exceptions to this policy must be approved by the policy owner and documented accordingly.
## 8. Record Keeping

- - - All transfer requests, approvals, and related communications must be retained in the administrative system for audit and reference purposes.
## 9. Review

- - - This policy will be reviewed annually or as needed to ensure ongoing relevance and effectiveness.
