# Policy on Pregnancy & Workshop Attendance

# Policy on Pregnancy & Workshop Attendance

**Department:** Workshops  
**Policy Owner:** Kaisey  
**Policy Origin:** Policy on Pregnancy & Workshop Attendance

---

## Purpose

This policy outlines the approach of Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) regarding the attendance of pregnant teachers at PBT Certification Teachers Workshops. The aim is to ensure the safety, comfort, and inclusion of pregnant participants, while providing clear guidance to staff and tutors on how to support these attendees appropriately.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All PBT Certification Teachers Workshops (including all levels and PCT Teachers Certification Workshops).
- All teachers and participants who are pregnant and wish to attend a workshop.
- All PBT workshop tutors, area managers, and customer service staff involved in the organisation and delivery of workshops.

---

## Policy

### 1. Attendance of Pregnant Teachers

- Pregnant teachers are welcome and encouraged to attend PBT workshops.
- It is common for teachers to participate at various stages of pregnancy.
- Pregnant attendees are permitted to participate only in exercises they feel comfortable with and may opt out of any activities as needed.

### 2. Exercise Modifications and Safety

- Certain PBT exercises are not recommended during pregnancy.
- Variations of some exercises are available to accommodate pregnant participants.
- Pregnant attendees must inform the workshop tutor of their pregnancy upon arrival (or in advance if possible).
- Tutors are responsible for providing advice, offering suitable exercise modifications, and ensuring the comfort and safety of pregnant participants throughout the workshop.
- The health and well-being of the pregnant attendee and their baby is the highest priority.

### 3. Booking and Workshop Information

- Pregnant teachers should be advised to check workshop availability before booking.
- If a workshop is sold out, pregnant teachers may be added to the wait-list.
- If the workshop is within 5 days, customer service must advise the attendee of the required equipment before booking, to ensure adequate preparation time.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Pregnant Attendees:** Must communicate their pregnancy to the tutor at the start of the workshop (or earlier if possible) and participate only in exercises they are comfortable with.
- **Workshop Tutors:** Must provide guidance, exercise modifications, and support to pregnant attendees, ensuring their safety and comfort.
- **Customer Service Staff:** Must provide accurate information regarding workshop attendance for pregnant teachers, check availability, manage wait-lists, and advise on equipment requirements as appropriate.
- **Area Managers:** Must be informed if any special accommodations are required for pregnant attendees.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- While pregnant teachers are welcome, participation in all exercises is not mandatory. Attendees should always prioritise their own health and consult with their healthcare provider if unsure about participation.
- If a pregnant attendee has additional questions or specific needs, staff should provide tailored support and escalate to the area manager or workshop tutor as appropriate.
