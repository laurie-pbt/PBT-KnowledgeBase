# Workshop Previous Certification Verification

## Workshop Previous Certification Verification Policy

**Department:** Workshops  
**Policy Owner:** Kaisey  
**Source Document Section:** Workshops

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the requirements and procedures for verifying previous certification when participants register for Level 2 or Level 3 Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) Certification Teachers Workshops. Its purpose is to ensure that all workshop participants have completed the necessary prerequisite training, maintaining the integrity and progression of the PBT certification pathway.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All staff involved in the administration and management of PBT Certification Teachers Workshops (Levels 1, 2, and 3).
- All participants registering for Level 2 or Level 3 PBT Certification Teachers Workshops.
- All regions and delivery formats (in-person and online workshops).

---

## Policy

### 1. Prerequisite Certification Requirement

- Level 1 PBT certification is mandatory before a participant may attend a Level 2 or Level 3 PBT Certification Teachers Workshop.
- Participants registering for Level 2 or Level 3 workshops must provide evidence of having completed Level 1 certification.

### 2. Registration and Verification Process

#### a. Registration Monitoring

- The workshop registration system will automatically flag participants who register for Level 2 or Level 3 workshops without a record of Level 1 certification.

#### b. Request for Certification Evidence

- Participants flagged as not having a Level 1 certification on record will be automatically emailed and asked to provide their Level 1 certificate.

#### c. Certificate Submission

- Participants must submit a copy of their Level 1 certificate in response to the request.
- If the participant cannot provide a Level 1 certificate, staff must offer the following options:
  - Transfer the registration to an upcoming Level 1 workshop.
  - Move the registration to a future Level 2 or Level 3 workshop once Level 1 is completed.
  - Process a refund if neither option is suitable.

#### d. Certificate Verification

- Upon receipt of a Level 1 certificate, staff must:
  1. Verify the authenticity of the certificate.
  2. Navigate to the participant’s workshop booking in the admin system.
  3. Select Actions > “Verify previous certification” > “Verify and confirm.”
- Only after successful verification will the participant receive their workshop confirmation and ticket.

#### e. Workshop Confirmation

- Once previous certification is verified, the system will automatically send the workshop confirmation and ticket to the participant.

### 3. Non-Compliance

- Participants who do not provide valid evidence of Level 1 certification will not be permitted to attend Level 2 or Level 3 workshops.
- Staff must ensure that no exceptions are made unless specifically authorised by the Workshops department manager.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Workshop Administration Staff:**  
  - Monitor registrations for prerequisite compliance.
  - Communicate with participants regarding certification requirements.
  - Verify submitted certificates for authenticity.
  - Update the admin system to reflect verification status.
  - Offer appropriate alternatives (transfer, deferment, refund) if prerequisites are not met.

- **Policy Owner (Kaisey):**  
  - Oversee implementation and compliance with this policy.
  - Authorise any exceptions or updates to the verification process.
  - Provide guidance and support to staff as needed.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- This policy applies only to PBT Certification Teachers Workshops (Levels 1, 2, and 3).  
- PCT (Progressing Contemporary Technique) workshops do not require PBT certification as a prerequisite.
- In exceptional circumstances (e.g., administrative error, system failure), the Workshops department manager may approve a manual override, provided the participant’s prior certification can be otherwise verified.
- All communications with participants regarding certification verification must be professional, clear, and supportive.
