# RedNote and Social Monitoring Policy

# RedNote and Social Monitoring Policy

**Department:** Customer Service  
**Policy Owner:Ruby

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) China customer service staff consistently monitor and manage all RedNote and relevant social channels as part of their official duties. This policy establishes clear standards for monitoring, responding, and nurturing customer engagement across these platforms, ensuring timely support, issue resolution, and a positive customer experience in all customer-facing digital spaces.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all PBT China customer service staff, including those providing workshop support, China marketing support, and morning/evening shift coverage. It covers all activities related to monitoring, responding, and engaging with customers on RedNote groups, social media groups, direct messages (DMs), comments, and any other customer-facing social channels assigned to China service coverage.

---

## Policy

### 1. Monitoring Responsibilities

- **Shift Coverage:**  
  While on shift, customer service staff must actively monitor the following customer-facing surfaces:
  - RedNote groups and channels
  - Social media groups relevant to PBT China
  - Social media direct messages (DMs) and comments
  - Other live customer-facing social channels assigned to China service coverage

- **Visibility:**  
  Staff must have all relevant windows and platforms open during their shift to ensure visibility and prompt response. This includes:
  - Main WeCom customer service channel
  - Workshop groups
  - PBT China Community Group
  - PBT Workshop Interest Group
  - RedNote groups/channels
  - Social media inboxes and comments requiring support
  - Alice (internal support tool)
  - Current workshop schedule

### 2. Order of Priority

- **First Priority:**
  - Monitor all assigned channels for customer activity.
  - Spot and address direct customer questions, complaints, booking issues, or confusion immediately.
  - Respond promptly and professionally to any customer enquiry or issue.

- **Second Priority:**
  - Nurture relationships by encouraging conversation and supporting soft conversion (e.g., inviting customers to join groups, providing helpful information).
  - Build warmth and engagement in the space when there are no urgent issues pending.

### 3. Response Standards

- **Professionalism:**  
  All responses must be friendly, calm, helpful, professional, and solution-led.
- **Timeliness:**  
  Staff must reply as quickly as possible to maintain high service standards.
- **De-escalation:**  
  In the event of a complaint or negative comment, staff must de-escalate the situation, remain calm, and move towards resolution.
- **Positive Language:**  
  Use warm, positive, and supportive language. Avoid blunt, negative, or defensive wording.
- **No Guessing:**  
  If unsure about an answer, staff must confirm internally before responding.

### 4. Channel-Specific Guidelines

- **RedNote Groups:**  
  Staff must monitor all RedNote groups assigned to customer service coverage, respond to customer queries, and escalate issues as needed.
- **Social Media Groups and DMs:**  
  Monitor and respond to all relevant group discussions, direct messages, and comments. Where appropriate, encourage customers to use official support channels for ongoing issues.
- **Community and Workshop Interest Groups:**  
  Encourage customers to join these groups when relevant, supporting ongoing engagement and conversion in a natural, non-intrusive manner.

### 5. Handover and Shift Change

- At the end of a shift, ensure that all unresolved issues on RedNote and social channels are clearly handed over to the next staff member.
- Directly notify the next rep of any ongoing cases, providing necessary context to ensure continuity of service.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
  - Monitor, respond, and nurture engagement across all assigned RedNote and social channels during their shift.
  - Maintain visibility on all required platforms.
  - Escalate unresolved or complex issues according to the internal escalation paths.
  - Ensure smooth handover of ongoing cases at shift change.

- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
  - Oversee implementation and compliance with this policy.
  - Review and update the policy as needed to reflect operational changes.

- **Head of Customer Service China:**  
  - Support staff in managing social monitoring responsibilities.
  - Address escalated issues and ensure service standards are maintained.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- Monitoring and response duties only apply during official customer service hours and assigned shifts.
- Staff are not expected to provide active support outside their scheduled shifts unless specifically arranged.
- In periods of unusually high customer volume, more than one staff member may be active on the same channels to ensure adequate coverage.
