# Refunds, Discounts and Promotions Policy

## Refunds, Discounts and Promotions Policy

**Department:** Customer Service  
**Policy Owner:Ruby
**Source Document:** "Refunds, Discounts and Promotions"

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes clear, consistent guidelines for handling customer requests regarding refunds, discounts, and promotions for Progressing Ballet Technique (PBT) China. It ensures all staff provide fair, transparent, and professional responses, supporting customer trust and organisational integrity while protecting the business from unauthorised offers or exceptions.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all PBT China customer service staff, workshop support, China marketing support, and any team members involved in customer-facing interactions regarding sales, bookings, or enquiries about refunds, discounts, or promotional offers. It covers all scenarios relating to PBT workshops, memberships, and merchandise within the China region.

---

## Policy

### 1. Refunds

- **General Rule:**  
  Refunds are not automatically granted for customers who have already booked, even if more people join after their booking and become eligible for a group discount.
- **Alternative Value:**  
  In some situations, instead of a refund, an alternative form of value (such as an approved membership benefit) may be offered at management’s discretion.
- **Escalation:**  
  Refunds and exceptions outside the standard process require management judgement and approval. Staff must escalate such requests to the appropriate escalation owner.
- **Communication:**  
  Staff must not promise or process refunds without prior approval. When uncertain, staff should respond with:  
  “I understand why you’re asking. Let me check what we can approve in this situation.”

### 2. Discounts

- **Workshop Group Discount:**  
  If three or more people join the same workshop together, they are eligible for a 10% discount on that workshop.
- **Eligibility:**  
  The group discount applies only when bookings are made together for the same workshop.
- **No Retroactive Discounts:**  
  Customers who have already booked are generally not eligible for a retroactive discount if additional people join after their booking.
- **Explanation to Customers:**  
  Staff may explain the approved group discount rule but must not create or offer additional discounts beyond what is officially approved.

### 3. Promotions

- **Authorised Promotions Only:**  
  Only officially approved promotions may be communicated or offered to customers.
- **No Unauthorised Offers:**  
  Staff must not invent, promise, or imply any new offers, discounts, or exceptions that have not been formally approved by management.
- **Escalation of Special Requests:**  
  Any customer request for a promotion or discount outside the standard rules must be escalated for management review and approval.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Customer Service Staff:**  
  - Clearly communicate the approved refund, discount, and promotion rules to customers.
  - Escalate any requests or situations that fall outside the standard process to the appropriate escalation owner.
  - Never promise or process unauthorised refunds, discounts, or promotions.
- **Escalation Owners:**  
  - Review and decide on exceptional refund, discount, or promotion requests.
  - Ensure decisions are communicated back to the customer service staff promptly.
- **Policy Owner (Christian):**  
  - Maintain and update this policy as required.
  - Ensure all staff are trained and informed of any changes to refund, discount, or promotion rules.

---

## Notes / Exceptions

- **Management Discretion:**  
  In exceptional cases, management may approve a refund, alternative value, or special discount outside the standard policy. Such exceptions must be documented and communicated to all relevant staff.
- **Customer Communication:**  
  Staff must always use warm, professional, and positive language when discussing refunds, discounts, or promotions, and avoid blunt or negative wording.
- **No Invention of Offers:**  
  Under no circumstances may staff invent or offer unapproved refunds, discounts, or promotions.

---

This policy ensures all customer service interactions regarding refunds, discounts, and promotions are handled consistently, fairly, and in line with PBT China’s standards and business requirements.
