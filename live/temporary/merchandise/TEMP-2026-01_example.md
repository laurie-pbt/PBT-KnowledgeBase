# Summary
During the holiday surge, agents may waive one late fee per customer.

# When it applies
Use this policy when handling support tickets related to late fees during the surge period.

# Rules
- Only one late fee waiver per customer during the effective period.
- Document the waiver in the ticket notes.

# Allowed actions
- Waive a single late fee for eligible customers.
- Communicate the waiver and remaining balance.

# Disallowed actions
- Waive multiple late fees for the same customer.
- Apply the waiver outside the effective dates.

# Required wording
"As a one-time courtesy during the holiday surge, I can waive this late fee."

# Escalation
Escalate if the customer requests additional waivers or exceptions.

# Examples
A customer incurred a late fee on 2026-01-12. Waive the fee and note the exception.
