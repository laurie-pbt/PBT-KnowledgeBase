# Summary
Defines how PBT handles returns, refunds, exchanges, and faulty merchandise worldwide, subject to regional consumer laws.

# When it applies
All PBT Store merchandise purchases; regional overlays take precedence where they apply.

# Rules
- Change of mind: within 30 days; unused; original condition; tags and packaging intact.
- Faulty items: 12-month warranty for manufacturing defects; major fault means refund or replacement; minor fault means repair or replacement.
- Proof of purchase required (order email, receipt, or bank record).
- Customer must contact support before returning; photos for faults when possible.
- Return shipping: change-of-mind customer pays; faulty items PBT pays return and replacement shipping.
- Refund timing: refunds to original method within 5 business days after receiving the return; store credit may be faster.

# Allowed actions
- Provide refunds, exchanges, or store credit within policy limits.
- Request photos or other evidence of a fault.
- Offer goodwill solutions within approval limits.

# Disallowed actions
- Refuse legitimate warranty claims.
- Issue refunds without proof of purchase.
- Override consumer law rights.

# Required wording
"We're happy to help with a return or refund in line with our policy and your local consumer rights."

# Escalation
Legal disputes, unclear fault classification, or high-value orders.

# Examples
- A customer changes their mind within 30 days and the item is unused with tags intact; the return is eligible.
- A manufacturing defect appears after 6 months; the warranty applies and a remedy is required.
