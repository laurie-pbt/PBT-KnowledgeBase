# Summary
Shipping coverage, delivery estimates, carriers, duties, and tracking for merchandise orders.

# When it applies
All merchandise orders.

# Rules
- Rates are calculated at checkout; free shipping may apply if a threshold is met.
- Delivery times are estimates only; they are not guaranteed.
- Carriers vary by region (AusPost, DHL, UPS, FedEx, USPS, or local equivalents).
- Tracking is provided on dispatch; delays do not always mean a parcel is lost.
- Duties and taxes are the customer's responsibility unless DDP is selected at checkout.
- Lost or delayed: if the carrier confirms loss, offer a replacement or refund; delays alone do not qualify for an automatic refund.

# Allowed actions
- Help customers track shipments and interpret tracking updates.
- Open carrier investigations when deliveries appear delayed or missing.

# Disallowed actions
- Guarantee delivery dates or transit times.
- Cover duties or taxes unless explicitly authorized.

# Required wording
"Delivery times are estimates provided by carriers and may vary depending on location and customs processing."

# Escalation
Confirmed loss cases or systemic carrier delays.

# Examples
- A parcel is held by customs; advise the customer about estimated delays and duties.
- The carrier confirms a shipment is lost; offer a replacement or refund.
