# Summary
We allow refunds for clear billing errors or service outages within the last 30 days.

# When it applies
Use this policy when a customer requests a refund for PBT services.

# Rules
- Refunds require evidence of a billing mistake or documented outage.
- Partial refunds are preferred when only part of the service was impacted.
- Refunds beyond 30 days need escalation approval.

# Allowed actions
- Approve full refunds for billing errors within 30 days.
- Approve partial refunds for limited outages within 30 days.

# Disallowed actions
- Grant refunds without documented evidence.
- Approve refunds beyond 30 days without escalation.

# Required wording
"I can help with a refund for a verified billing issue or outage within the last 30 days."

# Escalation
Escalate to the Support Lead for exceptions or refunds beyond 30 days.

# Examples
A customer was double-charged last week. Approve a full refund for the duplicate charge.
